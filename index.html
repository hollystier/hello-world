Harrisonburg, VA- Friendly By Nature

Harrisonburg, VA is home to many. A modge-podge of backgrounds, interests, and ages creates a friendly atmostphere that can feel like home for nearly anyone. Our charming downtown can make for a relaxing sunday or a fun night out. A walk through James Madison University's campus on a game day can bring the excitement. A day spent hiking through Shenandoah can provide epic views. Don't forget about the cool finds at the local farmers market. But regardless, the people are who make it feel like home.

Between the local small businesses, JMU activities, and the surrounding natural beauty, there's something for everyone here. Take some time to learn about our downtown or the local college campus, JMU. What are you waiting for? We can't wait to meet you.
